About Karnataka

Karnataka is home to UNESCO World Heritage sites like Hampi and Pattadakal, royal landmarks such as Mysuru Palace, scenic hills of Coorg and Chikkamagaluru, and the majestic Jog Falls. The state offers a perfect blend of heritage, adventure, wildlife and natural beauty, making it one of India's most diverse travel destinations.
